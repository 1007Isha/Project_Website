Who We Are

The Farmers' Market exists to generate fresh, wholesome connections, beginning with the one between the farmer and the market. We start by creating a market space so these networks can grow to become a essential part of the community. Our mission here at the Farmers Market is to cultivate this tradition by maintaining convenience, accessibility and diversity for farmers who expect it to be there. It’s also part of our mission to preserve a sense of place that’s distinctly flavored with the essence of the local communities we serve.

Our Purpose

Markets are unpredictable and can greatly impact the farmers when times get tough.Advocating or raising awareness about farmers' condition can help strengthen a community.We take pride in providing the community with electronic connectivity to the wholesale markets of the country in a comfortably accessible, user-friendly environment. The objective is to collect, analyse and disseminate market information to the farmers, traders, Policy makers and other stakeholders.

Future Plans

In future we aim to improve our quality of service by providing reasonable mandi prices of not only specific crops but also majority of crops available in the market according to the preference of the farmers and the dealers.Everyday, you’ll find an abundant showcase of fruits, vegetables, and crops ready to take from the market. Accurate Market Information about the price and arrival etc. for the agricultural produce will be available to the farmers taking proper production and marketing decisions.
